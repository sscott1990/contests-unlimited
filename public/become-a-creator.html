How It Works

  1. Submit your contest idea (it’s free!) — choose a theme, contest type, add a description, set a password.
  2. Pick your contest duration: 1 month, 3 months, 6 months, or 1 year.
  3. We review & approve your contest—usually within 24 hours.
  4. Share your contest link — every valid entry earns you 25% of the entry fee up to the minimum, and 30% for every entry above the minimum.
  5. We handle payments, entries, winner selection, and prize payouts. You focus on sharing your contest and watching your earnings grow!
  6. Prize pot gets a guaranteed seed ($250–$1,000) if your contest meets the minimum entries by deadline.
  7. You get paid your full earnings after the contest closes and the winner is verified—typically within 7–14 business days.

Eligibility, Payments & Tax Requirements:
  • Contest creators must be U.S. citizens, legal residents, or legal entities (such as businesses) with a valid U.S. tax identification number (SSN, TIN, or EIN).
  • Before any creator payments totaling $600 or more in a calendar year can be issued, you must submit a completed IRS Form W-9. Failure to provide required tax documentation will result in forfeiture of creator payments exceeding this threshold. Creators (contest hosts) have up to 30 calendar days from notification to submit their W-9 before forfeiting their payment.
  • All creator earnings are reported in accordance with IRS regulations.
